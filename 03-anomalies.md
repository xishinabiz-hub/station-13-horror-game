### P-02: THE REFLECTION-LESS (Medium)
Businessman. NO REFLECTION in booth glass. CCTV: Dark shadow standing behind them. Photo: Blurry/smeared. Ticket: COMPLETELY BLANK white card.

### P-03: THE STRETCHED (Medium)

### P-08: THE BACKWARDS (Med/Hard)

### P-10: THE MANNEQUIN (Medium)

### P-11: THE WET (Easy)

### P-14: THE WRONG SEASON (Easy)

# SECTION B: 10 HAZARD ANOMALIES

### H-01: FLESH CEILING
Pulsating flesh with eyes on ceiling. Drips fluid. "DON'T LOOK UP" warning. Looking = -2%/s sanity. Avoid looking up, retracts in 45-90s.

### H-02: TRANSLUCENT MAN
Near-invisible humanoid (heat haze). Out-of-sync breathing. CCTV shows grey silhouette. Touch = -15% + teleport. Avoid. Can't enter Control Booth.

### H-03: TRIPLE FLICKER (Rule #5)
Lights flicker 3× fast → 30s TOTAL DARKNESS. Flashlight WON'T WORK. RUN to booth, CROUCH under desk before darkness. Fail = -25% + jump scare.

### H-04: GHOST TRAIN
Train sound + headlights from tunnel. LOOKING = -30% + 60s ghost hallucinations. AVERT EYES. Sound peaks → silence. Tracks empty. Horn sounds like human scream.

### H-05: PA DISTORTION
Creepy PA messages. Sometimes player's own voice "Help me" (-10%). Sometimes helpful clue. Can't avoid.

### H-06: DOOR KNOCKER
LOUD banging on sealed door. Each bang = -2%. Approach makes it worse. STAY AWAY. Stops 8-15s.

### H-07: WEEPING WOMAN
Sobbing from restrooms. Woman in tattered dress facing corner. Approach = she turns → NIGHTMARE FACE (-20%, teleport). IGNORE. Don't enter restrooms. Dress = same uniform as player.

### H-08: SHADOW PERSON
Pure black 3D silhouette, no features. Stare 5s+ → TELEPORTS CLOSER. Repeats. Reach = -30%. DON'T STARE. Break LOS. Walk away. Vanishes 10s out of sight.

### H-09: WEEPING ANGEL
Marble statue. Only MOVES WHEN NOT OBSERVED. Blinks = teleports ~3 studs closer. POSE changes each move. KEEP EYES ON IT. Back to Control Booth (safe zone).

### H-10: BLACKOUT
Complete power failure (30-60s). All lights dead. ALL hazards active simultaneously. Flashlight attracts attention. Go to booth. Stay still. Power returns: 10s full bright flash → normal.
